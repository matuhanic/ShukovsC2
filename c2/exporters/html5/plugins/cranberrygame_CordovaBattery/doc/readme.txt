5.API

//events
On battery status (only supported in android, blackberry10, ios, wp8)
On battery low (only supported in android, blackberry10, ios)
On battery critical(only supported in android, blackberry10, ios)

//expressions
BatteryStatusLevel: Get battery status level percent. (0-100)
BatteryStatusPlugged: Check battery status plugged. (0 or 1)
BatteryCriticalLevel: Get battery critical level percent. (0-100)
BatteryLowLevel: Get battery low level percent. (0-100)
